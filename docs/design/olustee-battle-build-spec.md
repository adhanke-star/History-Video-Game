## 1. The design law in one paragraph

The scenario is the MEETING ENGAGEMENT in the pine corridor — a single-phase fight in
which Finegan's concentrated Confederates break Seymour's piecemeal-committed line, an
HONEST UNION DEFEAT the engine must be allowed to produce from true inputs (exposed
arrival schedules against a defense-turned-attack), never force with a winner gate. The
AFTERMATH — the documented killing of Black wounded left on the field — is never in the
scenario: no aftermath event, mechanic, imagery, casualty script, or scoring exists
anywhere in the battle data; the record is taught with its scale honestly CONTESTED, and
any post-battle atrocity choice resolves ONLY through the shipped D457 no-quarter
machinery. The rearguard that saved the column is the teaching heart: valor in defeat —
dignity does not require victory (the D382 women-arc precedent). The runtime commit adds
ZERO engine code — data + registry + teeth.
