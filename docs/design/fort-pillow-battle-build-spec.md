The scenario is the ASSAULT — a single-phase attacker-seize on the fortified bluff, built
from true inputs, ending at the works. The MASSACRE is never in the scenario: no massacre
event, mechanic, imagery, casualty script, or scoring exists anywhere in the battle data.

which is scenario-agnostic and already runtime: captures stamp `C.mayhemNoQuarterOffer`

a red there is a design failure, never a tooth to move); Mayhem takes its chartered reward
action; declining leaves the campaign byte-identical. The runtime commit adds ZERO engine

inner fort on the bluff as the objective with its ditch/parapet (the parapet dimensions

no gunboat mechanic (the no-naval-engine law).
